CompanionDogs: Asset Credits & Licenses
========================================

Sounds
------
Dog voices (bark / growl / whine / idle / death): media/sound/CDDog*.ogg
Source: BigSoundBank.com (Joseph Sardin), CC0 (public domain).
Attribution optional; credited with thanks.
The growl (CDDogGrowl) is made from "Growling cat" (ID 1886 / 1887),
pitch-shifted down with high/low-pass EQ into a deep dog growl.
Clips cut/converted to mono 44.1 kHz with ffmpeg.

Eating / drinking foley: media/sound/CDDogEat1.ogg, CDDogDrink1.ogg
Source: Freesound.org, CC0 (public domain).
Eating: "a dog eats his kibble" by aisselles (#725619).
Drinking: "Dog drinking water" by artemditkovsky (#860331).
Cut/converted to mono 44.1 kHz with ffmpeg. Credited with thanks.

Bite foley reuses the vanilla "ZombieBite" sound.

Models
------
German Shepherd body mesh: our own 3D model.
Re-skinned to a custom Biped skeleton and adapted for the game.

Skeleton + skinning: original work.
Pipeline tooling (Blender headless + io_directx_x): in _dogrig/ (dev only).
