Changelog 2025:
So! build 42 unstable is here, hooray! There are a few new things i've added, but for the most part, the focus was on porting the mod to b42.

[b] New Things: [/b]
- You can now see and read fortune messages.
- Added Blood Sausages that can be made with animal blood. (thanks Turbotowns)
- Added Minced Pork and Turkey.
- Added Fried Chicken Wings and Fried Whole Chicken.

[b] Tweaks: [/b]
- Tweaked several recipes, for less bloat.
- Tweaked the protein value of Protein Powder(from 600 to 400). (maybe this'll help you Uppy?)
- Removed several items that are now vanilla! (Gin, Vodka, Rum, Tequila, Vermouth, Ice Cream Sandwiches, Pizza Cutter, Steel Ladle, Metal Whisk, Cola)
