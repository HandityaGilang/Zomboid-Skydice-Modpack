- Changelog 2026 - 
Happy new year, wishing you all a happy and productive 2026, let's hope this year isn't as chaotic as 2025's~
I was able to add some new things, and fixes, so i hope everyone's able to enjoy it
(if youre on 42.12 i'll be updating that version later this week!)

[b] New Things: [/b]
- Added Copper Saucepan recipes for Arborio Rice, Brown Rice, Popcorn Kernels, Spaghetti, Chocolate and Instant Noodles.
- Added recipes for Swiss Roll.
- Added recipes for Boxed Canned Foods.
- Added recipes for Cheesecakes (Vanila, Chocolate, Green Tea and Strawberry).
- Added recipes for Puffed Rice.
- Added recipes for Crispy Rice Squares.
- Added recipes for Cornbread Pan and Cornbread.
- Added recipes for Deviled Eggs.

[b] Tweaks: [/b]
- Added Small Bird Meat on Fry Chicken recipe (thanks Llareia).
- Fixed a bug with Pour on Thermos recipe missing food (thanks Cannon Fodder).
- Fixed Takeout Box returning wrong pan in recipe (thanks Llareia).
- Fixed a bug with item tags being accepted in other recipes (thanks Kopiko).
- Fixed not learning mod recipes when reading magazines.
- Readded Cake Candles recipes.
