Previous system:
- Fixed chances of 20% for a perfectly preserved brain, 30% for medium quality, and 60% for low quality — regardless of profession.

New system:
- A Lab Intern with level 10 in Butchering and First Aid can reach up to 50% chance of obtaining perfectly preserved brains. Doctors can reach up to 40%. Other professions can reach up to 30%. This cap increases slightly with the Science skill (requires the mod installed).

Gameplay now converges toward encouraging specialized builds for laboratory research (just like players create specific builds for wilderness survival or combat) reinforcing scientist/doctor roleplay.
